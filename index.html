Lavender

Botanical Name: Lavandula angustifolia

Benefits: Lavender is widely known for its calming and soothing effects. It is often used for reducing anxiety, aiding sleep, and treating minor burns or insect bites.

Usage: Can be used in aromatherapy, as an essential oil, or in teas and topical ointments.
